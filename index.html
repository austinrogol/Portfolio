Information About Me

Creating commercial patterns that are produced as physical products is my passion. I love conceiving patterns from imagination and working with great brands to bring them into reality. Each day, an important part of my life is entering into my studio, connecting with my inspiration, and producing patterns that people enjoy having in their life.
